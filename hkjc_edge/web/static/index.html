What this is — and isn't

HKJC Edge Lab is a personal research tool that honestly tests whether a public-data model can beat the HKJC closing line. The current verdict is NO-GO — no validated edge — so the default action everywhere is NO BET.

It is not betting advice, not a tipster, and not affiliated with the HKJC. Simulated results are hypothetical and after-takeout. You are expected to lose the ~17.5% takeout on average. Bet at your own risk and within the law (Cap.148).

Turn the edge gate ON?

The validation verdict is NO-GO. Turning this on makes the tool emit bet suggestions the evidence does not support. These bets are expected to lose the ~17.5% takeout on average. This is for research/replay only and resets to OFF on relaunch.
